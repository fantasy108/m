Zecheng DOU

Zecheng Dou was born on Jan 22nd, 1997 in Henan, China. He grew up in Beijing and started learning golf when he was seven years old. At present, DOU has become a member of China National Amateur Golf Team. He began to play world level amateur or junior events in US from 2012. In 2013, Dou made the cut in Volvo China Open, become the youngest player in the history of VCO.

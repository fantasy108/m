Cheng Jin

Jin was born on March 6nd, 1998 ,Beijing, China. Cheng Jin is a member of the China national golf team. He began to play golf when he was eight, after two years moved to Singapore with family in 2008. Jin participated in HSBC China Junior Open in 2010. Following a first, second and third Round of 76, 73 and 78. At present, Jin has become a member of China National Amateur Golf Team. In 2014 Nine Dragons Open, Cheng Jin opened the door to history as he became the first amateur to win a PGA TOUR China event. In 2015 Asia Pacific Amateur Championship, Jin qualified for the 2016 Masters Tournament by winning the 2015 Asia-Pacific Amateur Championship in Hongkong.

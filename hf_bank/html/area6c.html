WGC - HSBC Champions

The HSBC World Golf Champions in Shanghai is our flagship event in the men’s game. Shanghai is a special place for the bank. We opened for business there in 1865 – almost 150 years later it is still central to HSBC.The HSBC Champions, created in November 2005, attracts the world's best players and in 2009 the event was granted World Golf Championship status – the first outside the US to achieve this.

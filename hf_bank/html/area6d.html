HSBC Women’s Champions

The HSBC Women’s Champions showcases the best players in the women’s game. Now in its fifth year, the event is often described as ‘Asia’s Major’. Players qualify by winning tournaments on the LPGA Tour or by finishing in the top 20.HSBC Women's Champions serves as a platform to host our best corporate and private customers in a country where we have a long and distinguished heritage.

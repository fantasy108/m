HSBC Junior Golf Camps

HSBC Junior Golf Camps have entry-level and advanced camps designed for 0 level kids and national junior team or top players respectively during summer and winter holiday. The camps have introduced golf to more than 8000 kids and provided them a chance to swing and put on site each year. The advanced camps provide systematic training courses on physical skills, on course skills, rules, strategies etc. to help the top players develop their potentials to be a pro from all around.

HSBC National School Training Program

Since 2007, HSBC National School Training Program has been launched in nearly 100 schools in Shanghai, Beijing, Jiangsu, Anhui, Shenzhen, Zhejiang, Shandong, Zhengzhou, Hainan and Yunnan province, setting up golf fundamental education during Physical Education (PE) classes in schools. This project is intended to introduce golf to more kids as a sport and provide them with a chance to play, understand and love golf.
